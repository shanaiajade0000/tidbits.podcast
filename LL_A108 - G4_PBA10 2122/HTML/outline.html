OUTLINE

Outlining in streaming podcast is writing and organizing ideas about your topic ahead of time so that when an event occur, you know where and what needs to discuss first. It will save you time and will deliver your event smoothly, as you have a clear idea of what will happen next.

Below is our team tidbits outline. Our team members decide on each column equally and accordingly to the Time, Topic of the stream, and Activities.

TIME (PST) TOPIC ACTIVITY A.T. (minutes) SCREEN MEDIA/ DEVICES
12:40 - 13:00 Collecting audiences Show standby video 20 mins Video Video Audio/ Microphone
13:00 - 13:10 Introduction of the team The host will have a short introduction of theirselves and will introduce the rest of the team 10 mins Screen camera and overlay Video Audio/ Microphone
13:10 - 13:30 Introduction to the topic The hosts will put a little game to engage with the audience 20 mins Slide Presentation Video Audio/ Microphone
13:30 - 13:50 Discussion of the topic Discuss facts and relevant information about the topic 20 mins Screen camera and overlay Video Audio/ Micrphone
13:50 - 14:15 Discussion with the audiences Discuss the sides and judgement of topic. and exchange opinions with the audience 25 mins Screen camera and overlay Video Audio/ Microphone
14:15 - 14:25 Summing Up Summarize learning and key points of the topic 10 mins Screen camera and overlay Video Audio/ Microphone
14:25 - 14:30 Outro Show outro video 5 mins Video Video Audio
